SPAGO, BEVERLY HILLS

BEVERLY HILLS

Spago is now open

Click here to make a dine-in reservation

Click here to order to-go and delivery

View our safety protocols here

Located in the heart of Beverly Hills along Canon Drive, Spago is the flagship restaurant of the Wolfgang Puck Fine Dining Group. Renovated in 2012 by Waldo’s Designs, the sleek and simple design of the restaurant enhances the iconic, market-driven menus and exceptional level of service for which the restaurant is best known. For lunch and dinner guests can choose from a seasonal a la carte menu, or explore the best of the west with the chefs’ multi-course California Tasting Menu.

DIRECTIONS

  • If you are traveling East on Wilshire Blvd. make a left on Beverly Dr., then make a right on Dayton Way. Travel one block East until you reach Canon Dr. and make a right onto Canon Dr. Make a U-turn at the bottom of Canon Dr. at the closure in order to access the Spago Beverly Hills valet.
  • If you are traveling west on Wilshire Blvd. make a right on N. Redford Dr., then a left onto Brighton Way. Continue going west on Brighton Way until you reach Canon Dr. Make a left turn on Canon Dr. and continue south on Canon Dr. until you reach the subway closure. Make a U-turn at the end of Canon Dr. in order to access the Spago Beverly Hills valet.

Closure General Information:

  • The intensity of the construction and police presence will lessen over the course of the next few weeks.
  • The closure at the corner of Canon Dr. and Wilshire Blvd. will remain up for approximately the next two years, as we await the exciting completion of the Metro Purple Line Station at the corner of Wilshire and Canon.
  • The closure allows for legal U-turns at the base of Canon Dr. allowing guests much easier access to Spago Beverly Hills.
